JOB TITLE: Recruiter / HR Specialist

LOCATION: Remote / Hybrid (Iasi)
EXPERIENCE: 2–5 years
EDUCATION: Human Resources, Psychology, Business, or related field

ROLE OVERVIEW:
We are looking for a Recruiter / HR Specialist to manage the full recruitment lifecycle and support hiring processes. You will work closely with hiring managers to identify talent needs and ensure a smooth candidate experience.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Manage end-to-end recruitment processes
- Source candidates through various channels (LinkedIn, job boards, referrals)
- Screen resumes and conduct initial interviews
- Coordinate interviews with hiring teams
- Maintain communication with candidates throughout the process
- Build and maintain talent pipelines
- Ensure a positive candidate experience
- Collaborate with hiring managers to define job requirements

REQUIRED SKILLS:
- Strong communication skills
- Interviewing and evaluation skills
- Experience with recruitment tools and platforms
- Organizational skills
- Ability to manage multiple roles at once
- Stakeholder management

NICE TO HAVE:
- Experience with ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
- Knowledge of employer branding
- Experience in technical recruitment
- Basic understanding of HR processes

TEAM & CULTURE:
- People-focused environment
- Collaborative team culture
- Emphasis on communication and transparency
