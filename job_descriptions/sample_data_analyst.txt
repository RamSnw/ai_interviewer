JOB TITLE: Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst to support reporting, data validation, and business insights.

Main responsibilities:
- Gather and clean data from different sources
- Build reports and dashboards
- Work with SQL queries to extract and validate data
- Analyze trends and communicate findings clearly
- Support business teams with ad hoc analysis

Required skills:
- Good SQL knowledge
- Experience with Excel or reporting tools
- Strong attention to detail
- Good communication skills
- Ability to work with structured data

Nice to have:
- Python knowledge
- Experience with dashboards such as Power BI or Tableau
- Experience working with stakeholders
