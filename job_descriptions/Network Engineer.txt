JOB TITLE: Network Engineer

LOCATION: Cluj-Napoca (Hybrid)
EXPERIENCE: 1–3 years
EDUCATION: Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field

ROLE OVERVIEW:
Design, implement, and support network infrastructure solutions to ensure secure, reliable, and high-performing connectivity across systems and environments.
Work with modern networking technologies to maintain and improve enterprise network operations.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Configure and maintain network devices such as routers, switches, and firewalls
- Monitor network performance and troubleshoot connectivity issues
- Support LAN, WAN, VPN, and wireless network environments
- Collaborate with infrastructure and security teams on network-related projects
- Assist with network upgrades, maintenance, and documentation
- Ensure network availability, security, and compliance with standards
- Stay up to date with networking technologies and best practices

REQUIRED SKILLS:
- TCP/IP networking
- Routing and switching
- Network troubleshooting
- Firewalls and VPNs
- DNS, DHCP, and VLANs
- Understanding of network security principles
- Strong problem-solving skills

NICE TO HAVE:
- Experience with Cisco, Juniper, or similar vendors
- Knowledge of wireless networking
- Familiarity with cloud networking concepts
- Basic scripting knowledge (Python, Bash, or PowerShell)
- Certifications such as CCNA or equivalent

TEAM & CULTURE:
- Fast-paced environment
- Focus on reliability, security, and continuous improvement
- Strong learning culture
